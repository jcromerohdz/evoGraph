About this

This is an experiment on interactive evolutionary computation where you're contributing by rating a population of drawings.

Each drawing is presented to many users and depending on their collective taste drawings are selected for reproduction or not. If a drawing is selected their DNA is passed to the next generation just like in natural selection. This technique will produce new drawings that share the DNA of two parents selected by you.

You can store drawings you like in one of your collections and share them with your friends. You can follow the history of any drawing. If you rate many drawings you will begin to see more drawings with the features you like.


We appreciate your participation in this experiment thank you very much everyone!.
